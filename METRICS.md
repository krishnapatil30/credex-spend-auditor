# Key Success Metrics (MVP)

1. **Conversion Rate (Lead Gen):** Percentage of users who calculate spend vs. those who actually submit their email.
2. **AI Accuracy:** Sentiment analysis of generated summaries to ensure financial advice remains "professional" and "logical."
3. **Latency:** Goal of <2s for the database insertion and <4s for the AI response.
4. **ROI Accuracy:** Verification that "Annual Savings" accurately reflects the 30% waste hypothesis in SaaS seat fragmentation.
