# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## 1. Target Audience (ICP)
Our Initial Customer Profile (ICP) consists of:
- **Seed to Series B Startups:** 20–200 employees.
- **Decision Makers:** CTOs, Engineering Managers, and Finance Ops.
- **Pain Point:** High monthly burn rate on "miscellaneous" SaaS subscriptions.

## 2. Distribution Channels
- **Product Hunt Launch:** A targeted launch to reach the tech-founder community.
- **LinkedIn Outbound:** Using the "Personalized Audit Summary" as a hook. Instead of a cold pitch, we send a partial audit: *"We noticed your team of 100 might be losing $20k/year on AI seats..."*
- **VC Partnerships:** Partnering with startup accelerators (like YC or Antler) to offer the Auditor as a "Burn-Rate Health Check" for their portfolio companies.

## 3. The Lead Magnet Loop
The Auditor acts as the "Top of Funnel." 
1. **The Hook:** Free, instant savings calculation.
2. **The Exchange:** User provides email to see the "AI Personalised Summary."
3. **The Conversion:** Credex sales team follows up with the saved data to offer a 1-click migration to a consolidated enterprise plan.
